# 5 Whys Analysis - Why CATC Document Request Portal Was Created

## Problem 1: Manual Document Request Process
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To replace manual paper-based document requests |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Students had to travel to campus to submit requests |
| 3. Why was travel a problem? | Time-consuming, especially for working students |
| 4. Why was time a concern? | Delayed document processing affected job applications |
| 5. Why did delays matter? | Students missed important deadlines and opportunities |

## Problem 2: Limited Payment Options
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To enable online payment processing |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Cash payments required physical presence |
| 3. Why was presence a problem? | Inconvenient for students abroad or in other cities |
| 4. Why needed remote access? | Modern students expect digital services |
| 5. Why does this matter? | Competitive institutions offer online services |

## Problem 3: Poor Tracking Visibility
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To provide real-time request tracking |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Students frequently called/visited for status |
| 3. Why were inquiries problematic? | Overburdened registrar staff |
| 4. Why was staff workload an issue? | Manual lookups took time away from processing |
| 5. Why does this matter? | Faster processing benefits everyone |

## Problem 4: No Centralized Records
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To digitize and centralize all document records |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Paper records were easily lost or damaged |
| 3. Why was record loss concerning? | Affected audit trails and student verification |
| 4. Why needed digital records? | Easier lookup, backup, and reporting |
| 5. Why does this matter? | Institutional compliance and accreditation |

## Problem 5: Inefficient TOR Processing
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To automate TOR/Transcript pricing and processing |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Manual calculation errors were common |
| 3. Why were errors problematic? | Revenue discrepancies and student complaints |
| 4. Why automate pricing? | Per-page pricing is complex and varies |
| 5. Why does this matter? | Accurate billing builds trust |

## Problem 6: Limited Delivery Options
| Why | Answer |
|-----|--------|
| 1. Why was this system created? | To integrate LBC delivery for remote students |
| 2. Why was that needed? | Students outside the city couldn't pick up documents |
| 3. Why was pickup difficult? | Required travel costs and time |
| 4. Why needed delivery? | Students relocated for work after graduation |
| 5. Why does this matter? | Documents needed for employment abroad |

## Root Purpose Summary
The CATC Document Request Portal was created to:
- **Digitize** the document request process
- **Empower** students with 24/7 online access
- **Streamline** operations for registrar staff
- **Modernize**CATC as a digital institution
- **Retain** students through better service
